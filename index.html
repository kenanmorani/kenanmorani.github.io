About

Aydin Ayanzadeh received a B.S degree in Computer Science from the University of Tabriz, Tabriz, Iran in 2016. Currently, he is graduate student at Applied Informatics programme in Informatics Institute at Istanbul Technical University, Istanbul, Turkey.

He was a researcher at SiMiT Lab and he is currently a member of the Signal Processing for Computational Intelligence Group (SPACING). His research focuses on deep learning, computer vision and image processing systems.
